Analytics

“In God we trust. All others must bring data.”

Northeastern University’s Business Analytics Club (NUBAC) hosts experiential workshops and educational panels in order to increase students’ knowledge of systems and opportunities in Business Analytics. In a world where the sheer amount of data is increasing at an exponential rate, business managers must make informed decisions to drive the company forward, improve efficiency and achieve organizational goals. Our organization is committed to providing students with not only the tools but the skills to partake in data-driven decision-making.
